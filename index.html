About SP Engineering

Your trusted partner in industrial engineering solutions with a commitment to excellence, safety, and innovation

SP Engineering stands as a trusted leader in the installation and assembly of industrial elevators, EOT cranes, and electrical systems. With our experienced team of professionals and unwavering commitment to quality and safety, we ensure every project exceeds industry standards while delivering innovative solutions that drive your business forward.

Vision & Mission

Driving the future of industrial engineering through innovation and excellence

Our Vision

To be the leading force in the industrial elevator and crane sector, empowering industries worldwide with reliable, innovative, and sustainable vertical mobility and lifting solutions that transform how businesses operate.

Our Mission

To establish SP Engineering as the gold standard provider of electrical services, specializing in installation and maintenance of FLP lifts, EOT cranes, and hoists—delivering unmatched reliability across oil & gas, petrochemical, and power sectors in full compliance with international standards.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive engineering solutions tailored to your industrial needs

Installation & Commissioning

Expert erection, testing, and commissioning of industrial lifts and EOT cranes with rigorous safety protocols. Our certified team ensures seamless deployment that meets all regulatory compliance standards.

Maintenance Services

Comprehensive maintenance solutions including Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), preventive care, and emergency breakdown services. We keep your equipment running at peak performance with minimal downtime.

Supply & Skilled Workforce

Supply of electrical components and deployment of trained manpower, including certified lift/EOT operators and technical staff.
